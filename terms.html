Common Questions

How does ScrapDish generate recipes?

ScrapDish analyzes ingredients in your pantry and generates AI recipes using those ingredients.

How do I scan ingredients?

Use the Ingredient Scanner in the app and point your camera at the item or barcode.

Why can't I see my ingredients?

Make sure you're logged in and your internet connection is active.

How do I delete ingredients?

Open your Digital Pantry and swipe left on the ingredient.
